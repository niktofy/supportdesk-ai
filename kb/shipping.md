# Shipping

## Delivery times
We ship worldwide from our EU warehouse. Standard delivery takes **3–5 business days** in Europe and **7–12 business days** internationally. Express delivery (DHL) takes 1–2 days in Europe and 3–5 days worldwide.

## Shipping costs
Standard shipping is **free for orders over €50**. Below that it costs €4.90 in Europe and €9.90 internationally. Express shipping costs €14.90 flat.

## Order tracking
As soon as your order ships you receive an email with a tracking link. Tracking updates can take up to 24 hours to appear after the label is created.

## Changing the delivery address
We can change the delivery address any time **before the order ships** — contact us with your order number. After shipping, the address can only be changed through the carrier's redirect service.
