# Payments

## Payment methods
We accept **Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay and Google Pay**. All payments are processed over encrypted connections; we never store your card number.

## Payment failed
If your payment fails, first check that your card supports online and international payments. Then try another browser or payment method. If money was taken but you got no confirmation email, it's an authorization hold — it releases automatically within 5 business days.

## Invoices
A VAT invoice is attached to your order confirmation email. Need it issued to a company? Add the company details at checkout under "billing address", or ask us to reissue it later.

## Discount codes
Discount codes are entered at checkout and can't be combined. If a code doesn't work, check its expiry date and minimum order value. Codes can't be applied retroactively to completed orders.
