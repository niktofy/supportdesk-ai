# Returns & Refunds

## Return policy
You can return any item within **30 days** of delivery, no questions asked. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Start a return from your account page or by replying to your order confirmation email.

## Refund timing
Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within **3 business days** to your original payment method. Banks can take another 3–5 days to show the money in your account.

## Exchanges
Want a different size or color? Choose "exchange" instead of "refund" when starting your return — the replacement ships as soon as the carrier scans your return package, so you don't wait for the full round trip.

## Damaged or wrong items
If your order arrived damaged or we sent the wrong item, send us a photo within 7 days — we'll ship a replacement immediately at no cost, and you can keep or donate the wrong item.
