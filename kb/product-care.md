# Product Care

## Warranty
All products carry a **2-year warranty** covering manufacturing defects. The warranty doesn't cover normal wear, accidental damage or unauthorized repairs. Claims need your order number and a photo of the issue.

## Care instructions
Clean with a soft, slightly damp cloth. Avoid harsh solvents and direct sunlight for long periods. For textile parts, hand-wash cold and air-dry flat.

## Spare parts
Spare parts are available for most products for at least 5 years after purchase. Contact support with your order number and the part you need — many small parts ship free.
